Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

How do I add a new student?

Go to the Students page and click the "+ Add Student" button. Fill in the student's details (name, grade, parent info) and click Save. A unique Student ID will be automatically generated.

How do I run an assessment?

Navigate to the student's profile from the Students page, then use the game-based assessment tools (drawing accuracy, reading, etc.) available in the assessment section. Results are automatically saved and linked to the student.

Can students log in on their own?

Yes! Students can use the Student Login page with their unique Student ID and your Consultant ID. They can then access their assigned exercises and games independently.

How do I export student data?

Go to Settings → Backup & Export. You can export your data in JSON or CSV format. The CSV format can be opened in Excel or Google Sheets for easy analysis.

How do I change my password?

Navigate to Settings → Privacy & Security. Enter your current password, then enter and confirm your new password. Passwords must be at least 8 characters long.

What do the dyslexia severity levels mean?

Mild: Minor reading difficulties that can be managed with light accommodations.
Moderate: Noticeable challenges requiring structured intervention and support.
Severe: Significant difficulties requiring intensive, multisensory teaching approaches and ongoing professional support.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing. Data is stored in a secure MongoDB database with access controls. You can manage your data sharing preferences in Settings → Backup & Export.
